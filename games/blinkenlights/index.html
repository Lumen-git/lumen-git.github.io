    The Museum of Blinkenlights was made for a 2 part project in my Introduction to Virtual Reality class during my time as an undergrad. The goal of the project was to build a virtual environment to showcase what we’ve been discussing in class as well as meeting a list of requirements (using animations, player interaction, UI elements, ect.). I chose to build a museum filled with random tidbits I find interesting: technology, art, games, and of course, cats. The museum has 6 exhibits, as well as various other bits scattered around. Each main exhibit can be tracked by clicking its respective button in the pause menu (accessed by pressing TAB). All assets and bits of text used are credited within the museum itself or in the credits tab of the pause menu. More info, version history, and my other work can be found on itch.io here.


    Contols:

    WASD - move

    Mouse - look (mouse sensitivity can be changed in settings)

    Tab - pause

    Z - zoom

    Left shift - Sprint
