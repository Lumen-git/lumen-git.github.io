>> Click here to play Futility <<

Futility is a text adventure about life, love, going through the motions, and meeting a cat. It plays in browser, and is best played on a larger screen.


Special thanks to Aaron Reed and his book 50 Years Of Text Games for making me love text games, Frank Lantz & Universal Paperclips for showing me a few GUI elements are all you need for a great game (and teaching me JavaScript), and Davey Wreden & The Beginner's Guide for making me fall in love with narrative games. Also thanks to the songwriters and poets below who's words inspired parts of the story.
