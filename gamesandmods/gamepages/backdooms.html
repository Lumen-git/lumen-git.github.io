Backdooms



I found this WAD on a floppy disk jammed under the floorboards. Can you escape the Back Dooms?

Backdooms was the first Doom WAD (and first game mod) I ever released. Inspired by the legend of the Backrooms, a mono-yellow nightmare of infinite rooms with seemingly no use. Backdooms is an exploration focused mod that takes place in the Backrooms. Winding halls, empty rooms, and the looping echo of Running From Evil MIDI music surronds you as you try to escape! Find the 4 exit signs, activate the exit buttons, and get back to Underhalls!

Primay download can be found on ModDb, and source can be found on GitHub.
